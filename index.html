How the estimate works

  1. If enabled, the Gold Pass discount is removed from the shelf price first.
  2. The app then extracts the VAT portion from that discounted VAT-inclusive price.
  3. It applies the configured SkipTax refund share to that VAT amount.
  4. The remaining Euro total is converted to GBP with the latest saved rate.

This is meant as a quick park-side estimate rather than a bank-perfect conversion.
